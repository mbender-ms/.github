# Romantasy-Reader-Report — Final Beta Reader Report

Synthesize all romantasy beta reading into a comprehensive reader report focused on romance, emotion, and trope satisfaction.

## When to Use

- After completing a full read-through (romantasy-read-through) and scene critiques (romance-scene-critique)
- As a standalone assessment for shorter works
- To produce a final romantasy-focused verdict before revision

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Being analytical when you should be emotional — this is a READER report, not a craft analysis
- ❌ Judging the book for not being pure romance — it's a genre blend; evaluate the romance within that context
- ❌ Being uncomfortable with honest heat assessment — romantasy readers expect and deserve frank discussion of intimate content
- ❌ Ignoring the romance-sci-fi integration — the best feedback identifies where the genre blend works and where it clashes
- ❌ Soft-pedaling — a kind but honest report serves the author far better than a polite but vague one
