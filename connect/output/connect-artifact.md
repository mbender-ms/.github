### Reflect on recent setbacks - what did you learn and how did you grow?

A recurring challenge this period was balancing high-frequency maintenance/UUF work with strategic scenario and innovation work. Initial attempts to run multiple complex scenarios in parallel led to significant rework and delayed quality. By shifting to a more serial, structured approach—batching similar work, using reusable templates, and setting clear review cadences—I improved flow and quality. I grew by shifting from reactive task handling to a more system-oriented delivery model that preserves room for forward-looking work even during heavy operational demand. I learned to ask deeper planning questions upfront, document review processes early, and estimate more realistically based on actual team capacity and competing priorities.
