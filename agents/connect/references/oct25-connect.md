## Reflect on recent setbacks - what did you learn and how did you grow?

One key learning from the Azure to AWS migration project was the importance of upfront clarity. We underestimated the iterative work required and initially planned to run three articles in parallel. This led to significant rework and delays. By shifting to a serial approach, we improved quality and flow, though we missed our deadline by six weeks. I’ve grown by learning to ask deeper questions at the start, document review processes and requirements early, and estimate more realistically. I now approach projects with a stronger focus on planning, collaboration, and adaptability—ensuring smoother execution and better outcomes.
